About us

I hate studying for exams, is there any facebook application for that? Life isn’t about finding yourself. Life is about creating yourself. I don’t describe myself as a Christian or religious, but i like to think that how i live my life is honest. I just find myself happy with the simple things. Appreciating the blessings god gave me. My daily routine: Get up, Be brilliant, Go back to bed, Repeat. Behind every deleted facebook & whatsapp account there is untold story in my life.. I can guarantee you won’t find anybody else like me. I respect those who respect me and forget those who forget me. Simple as that.

I am living large and taking charge! It’s not about you or him. It’s about me and my life. Don’t judge me until you know me. Don’t underestimate me until you challenge me.

I may not be perfect but I’m always me. Don’t like me? Cool, I don’t wake up every day to impress you. Don’t make a permanent decision for your temporary emotion. Be good and shall always see good in everything and everyone and even in yourself.
